# e-LinkAcre

## Vision
Our vision is to **connect Farmers, Government, and Buyers** in one transparent platform.  
We aim to strengthen agriculture as a driver of national development, ensuring fair trade, sustainable practices, and measurable contributions to GDP and exports.

## Government Benefits 

e-LinkAcre is more than a marketplace. It is a **national development tool**.  
By connecting farmers, buyers, and government, the platform helps:
- **[Boost GDP](ca://s?q=Explain_agriculture_contribution_to_GDP)** :Track agriculture’s real impact on the economy with accurate harvest data.  
- **[Increase Exports](ca://s?q=Explain_agriculture_exports)** : Identify surplus crops for international trade.  
- **[Support Policy](ca://s?q=Explain_agriculture_policy_planning)** : Use real-time production reports for subsidies and planning.  
- **[Ensure Food Security](ca://s?q=Explain_food_security_in_agriculture)** : Forecast harvests to balance supply and demand.  
- **[Improve Accountability](ca://s?q=Explain_government_monitoring_in_agriculture)** : District agronomist approvals provide reliable statistics.

## Farmer Benefits
e-LinkAcre empowers farmers by:
- **[Fair Market Access](ca://s?q=Explain_fair_market_access_for_farmers)** : Sell directly to buyers without middlemen.  
- **[Better Prices](ca://s?q=Explain_better_prices_for_farmers)** : Earn true value for crops based on real market demand.  
- **[Transparency](ca://s?q=Explain_transparency_in_farming)** : Clear visibility of buyers, government approvals, and harvest expectations.  
- **[Community Growth](ca://s?q=Explain_farmer_community_growth)** :Connect with other farmers and share sustainable practices.  
- **[Support Services](ca://s?q=Explain_farmer_support_services)** : Access government programs, agronomist guidance, and training.

## Buyer Benefits

## How to Use
1. Open `index.html` in a browser.  
2. Navigate using the menu bar to explore different sections.  
3. Farmers and buyers can check **Updates** for harvest details.  
4. Government staff can use their portal for approvals and monitoring.  
5. Sign up via `loginpage.html` to create an account.
